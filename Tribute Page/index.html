Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of the Indian Independence Movement

Gandhi led India to independence.

Timeline of his events/milestones.

  • 1869: He was born on October 2 in Porbandar, Kathiawar, India.
  • 1887: He Graduated from high school in Ahmedabad.
  • 1888: Went to London where he studied law for three years.
  • 1893: Landed a one year contract to perform legal services in South Africa.
  • 1906: Ghandi organized his first mass civil-disobedience campaign in South Africa.
